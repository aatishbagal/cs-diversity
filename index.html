Diversity in Uses of Artificial Intelligence

Introduction to AI and Diversity

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a versatile technology with applications across various fields, contributing to diversity in its uses.

Healthcare

AI is employed in healthcare for disease diagnosis, drug discovery, and personalized treatment plans.

E-commerce

AI enhances customer experiences through personalized recommendations and efficient supply chain management.

Education

AI aids in personalized learning, intelligent tutoring systems, and automating administrative tasks.
